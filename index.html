Banda Filarmónica Santa Maria Xochixtepec

With the help of Alberto Montellano Mazas, a well-respected musician, native of Villa Hidalgo Yalalag, Oaxaca, “Banda Filarmónica Santa Maria Xochixtepec” began their first music lessons in 1978. The first students who became part of the formation of the band were community members that settled in Los Angeles, California, along with eager first generation Xochixtepec Oaxaqueno- Americans, yearning to understand the traditional music that made their family so proud. And with time, the band began to thrive and interested children of settled migrants joined this wonderful tradition. To this day, the band is currently in their fourth generation of musicians who are learning music. The band is proud to say that it is formed by either natives or descendants of Santa Maria Xochixtepec.

Banda Yatzachi el Bajo, Los Angeles

San Baltazar Yatzachi el Bajo (YEB) is one of 25 municipalities that form the district of Villa Alta in the northern region of the Sierra Mountains of Oaxaca, Mexico. Today, the town of Yatzachi el Bajo has a population of approximately 90 people. This number is a result of heavy migration to other parts of Mexico and to the United States. The city of Los Angeles is today home to a thriving Oaxacan population, which includesmembers of the Yatzachi el Bajo community. The earliest arrivals from YEB date as far back as the 1950s. By the mid 1970s, they had established a tightknit YEB community that began working to aid their hometown as well as finding ways to preserve and celebrate their traditions in their new environment.

Maqueos Music Academy

At every stage where the music director Estanislao Maqueos has presented his philharmonic, he has created emotion, admiration and won the public’s hearts. Integrated by numerous children and young people, ranging from five to 20 years old, this band enjoys prestige. During the last years, Maqueos and his assemble has performed at different important events in Los Angeles and played along with well-known singers such as Susana Harp, Lorenzo Negrete, Georgina Menneses, La Santa Cecilia and Lila Downs. They have been under the direction of worldwide known directors such as Baltazar Chavarria from the conservatory of Mexico, Pedro Miguel Fajardo from the Canary Islands of Spain, and have had the privilege to be part of a course and perform with Faustino Diaz, considered one of the best musicians, and the best trombonist of the world.

These musicians enjoy an extensive repertoire adding to hundreds of songs of different genres. The Oaxaca native, Estanislao Maqueos began his music and band director career in 1995. With only seventeen years living in Los Angeles, California, Maqueos has directed and assembled various philharmonics, founded a music school and taught hundreds of musician of all ages, different states and other countries. Estanislao Maqueos also formed the first youth philharmonic band in the United States made up by first generation Oaxaqueños in the United States He has trained hundreds of musicians of all ages at the Maqueos Music Academy, which he established in 2005. At this school, students learn how to play music instruments such as: trumpet, clarinet, soprano, alto sax, tenor sax, Eb Horn, trombone, tuba, baritone, percussion, flute, etc. He also specializes in assembling new music bands.
